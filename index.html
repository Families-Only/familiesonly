About Me

Keanu Reeves is an actor, musician, philanthropist, and entrepreneur. Known for iconic roles in The Matrix, John Wick, and Speed, he has inspired audiences worldwide with his talent and humility. Beyond acting, he co-founded Arch Motorcycle Company, played bass for Dogstar, and actively supports charitable causes.

Past Projects & Movies

From thrillers to heartfelt dramas, Keanu Reeves has starred in Point Break, Constantine, Bill & Ted’s Excellent Adventure, and many more.

Upcoming Movies & Projects
